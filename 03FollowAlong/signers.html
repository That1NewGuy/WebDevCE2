Who were the Signers?

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers may include:
Thomas Jefferson
Role: Principal author of the Declaration.
State: Virginia
Legacy: 3rd U.S. President, founder of the University of Virginia.
John Adams
Role: Advocate for independence; helped draft the Declaration.
State: Massachusetts
Legacy: 2nd U.S. President; major Founding Father.
Benjamin Franklin
Role: Senior statesman and diplomat; helped edit the Declaration.
State: Pennsylvania
Legacy: Scientist, inventor, ambassador to France.
